Pizza Dough

  • 300g water (1 1/3 cups)
  • 30g olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • 30g sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • 15g salt (2 teaspoons)
  • 500g flour (4 cups, can use mixure of AP & bread)
  • 7g yeast (2 teaspoons)

Knead till a smooth dough is formed. Adjust water or flour levels as needed. Divide in half to make 2 balls. Proof for a few hours, punching down dough every ~40 min. Best if refrigerated overnight, up to a few days.
