Kombucha BBQ Sauce

Great use for extra homemade kombucha.

In a pot cook:

  • Olive oil
  • Half an onion diced
  • Garlic cloves minced
  • Red pepper flakes
  • S&P

Add then simmer:

  • 2 cups plain kombucha
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 chipotle minced
  • A few squirts of ketchup (to thicken)
Blend with immersion blender.
